The Neurobotix team was founded in 2019 at the National College "Mihai Viteazul" in Bucharest. For 7 years, we have been competing in the FIRST Tech Challenge, achieving consistent results. Throughout this journey, we have evolved by learning from our mistakes and overcoming challenges through teamwork and perseverance.

We discovered that success does not come only from competitions, but also from the close collaboration between team members and the passion for turning ideas into reality. Every challenge has given us the opportunity to develop our skills and strengthen our team spirit.

For us, robotics means a way to bring innovation closer to the community and to inspire other young people to discover the fields of science and technology. We believe that our work should have a real purpose, not just a result. This idea guides everything we do, and it is reflected in our projects and in our team motto: "We work for a cause, not for the applause."
